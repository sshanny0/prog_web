About

With ‘casual with a twist’ always in mind, there’s one thing that we promise all of you with every piece of our clothing—it has to be something that any woman with every body type and personality would proudly wear on a daily basis.

What's Toujours Bellé?

It was all started with a dream. In 2008, we decided to create something from nothing - a brand that was founded out of our love and passion for fashion. We've put our heart and soul into every step that we take along the way - from designing to producing, until the clothes are hanging neatly in our racks; ready to be delivered to you. 'Casual With A Twist' is how we like to describe our clothing; because simplicity is key, and less is always more. Our ultimate dream is to create a sustainable brand with local partners and most of all - to always provide the best for you. Because you are the inspiration for everything that we do. Be inspiring, be you. #LoveToujoursBellé
