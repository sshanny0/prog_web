WHICH SIZE SHOULD I WEAR?

We get it, buying clothes online may be difficult sometimes. It' always the age-old question - "will it really fit me?".
Well, we've got some tips on how to measure yourself so that you will find the clothes that will fit you the best!



To choose the size that will fit you the best, measure your body as follows:
  1. Chest:
    Measure around the fullest part of your chest.
  2. Waist:
    Measure around the smallest part of the waist.
  3. Hip:
    Measure 20cm down from that smallest part of your waist.

When it comes to our beloved denim bottoms, it can be somewhat complicated when you try to figure out your size. Measure yourself by following the steps below and find the best fit for you!

  1. Waist:
    Measure around the smallest part of the waist.
  2. Hip:
    Measure around about 20cm below the waist. Be steady and don't move the measuring tape up and down!
